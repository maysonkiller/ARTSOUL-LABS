# ARTSOUL LABS 

ARTSOUL LABS is an independent automation studio focused on Discord bots, Telegram bots, Web3 tools, AI workflows, and custom automation solutions.

The website serves as a portfolio, service showcase, and client acquisition platform. Visitors can explore completed and active projects, learn about available services, review the development process, and submit project inquiries directly through the contact form.

## Services

* Discord Bot Development
* Telegram Bot Development
* Web3 Automation
* AI Agents & Workflows
* Community Management Tools
* Wallet Verification Systems
* NFT & Marketplace Integrations
* Analytics Dashboards
* Custom Business Automation

## Featured Projects

### Community Guardian Bot

Discord moderation, anti-raid protection, wallet verification, role management, and ticket support.

### Finance Assistant Bot

Telegram-based personal finance assistant with reminders, reports, and AI-powered interaction.

### Web3 Lead Radar

AI-powered lead discovery platform that scans public sources for development opportunities.

### ArtSoul OS

Automation infrastructure for the ArtSoul ecosystem, including Discord integrations, marketplace alerts, auction tracking, and collector verification.
